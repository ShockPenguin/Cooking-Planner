## Instructions

1. Finely dice the onion and mince the garlic. Shred the lettuce and grate the cheddar. Cut lime into wedges.
2. Warm a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the beef and break it up with a wooden spoon, cooking until no longer pink. Drain excess fat if desired.
3. Add onion and garlic, cook 2 minutes until soft. Stir in the taco seasoning with a splash of water, and simmer until the mixture is glossy and clingy — about 3 minutes.
4. Warm the taco shells per the package (a few minutes in a 180°C / 350°F oven crisps them nicely).
5. Fill shells with beef, then top with lettuce, cheddar, and a spoon of sour cream. Serve with lime wedges.

## Notes

- If you skip the seasoning packet: cumin (1 tsp), chili powder (1 tsp), smoked paprika (1/2 tsp), oregano (1/2 tsp), salt.
- Soft corn tortillas work just as well as hard shells.
